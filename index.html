AI | Data | Public Health — bridging it all together

I’m Hillary Kipruto, a health systems specialist and current Public Health doctoral student at the University of Pretoria. I work at the intersection of AI, data systems, and public health to help teams turn messy, multi-source health data into clear, decision-ready intelligence.

Over the past 10+ years I’ve helped design, deploy, or improve health information systems, digital health tools, and data standards across African settings. My strength is bridging analytics, project management, and organizational change so that data products are actually used.

This portfolio documents how I’m applying Generative AI to accelerate report drafting, metadata creation, policy notes, literature reviews, and dashboards.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week, live cohort bootcamp on no-code AI tools for task automation. It combines weekly workshops, live help sessions, private community forums, personalised feedback, capstone coaching, and a shareable certificate.

I’m using it to push my public health work toward AI | Data | Public Health — faster documentation, smarter literature scans, and AI-assisted data products.

Bootcamp features

Weekly live sessions, private community, feedback on assessments, capstone coaching, lifetime tutorial access.

What it teaches

Generate sites, docs, and decks; automate analysis; build research chatbots; and ship a final AI project.

Module 1 · Websites, Documents & Presentations

How LLMs work, generating Markdown/HTML, building simple research sites, AI for decks.

Module 2 · AI-Automated Data Analysis

Use ChatGPT Code Interpreter/Julius to process datasets, create automated reports & dashboards.

Module 3 · GenAI for Research & Text

AI-assisted literature reviews, text classification, summarisation and knowledge maps.

Module 4 · Custom LLM Chatbots

Build RAG-style assistants and connect them to knowledge bases for research work.

Module 5 · AI for Task Automation

Set up email automations, AI agents, and browser-based flows for repetitive research/admin tasks.

Module 6 · GenAI for Communication

Generate multi-audience summaries, visuals, video abstracts and scheduled content.

Module 7 · Capstone & Showcase

Build and present a working AI solution with real-time feedback.

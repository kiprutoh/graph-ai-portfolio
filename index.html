Capstone project

AFRO Analytics — AI-augmented indicator platform

As part of my Generative AI for Work & Research coursework, I am building an analytics platform that brings together curated health indicators for African countries and layers in AI and ML functionality for narrative summaries, trend exploration, and rapid “what does this mean?” insights.

The current prototype is browser-based and built in Streamlit. It is a living experiment in how we can combine:

  • clean indicator datasets and regional views,
  • simple, transparent analytics workflows, and
  • Generative AI components that help teams move from numbers to narratives.
